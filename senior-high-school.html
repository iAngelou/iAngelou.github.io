SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Tangub City National High School is a government institution of learning, hence; it attempts to be responsive to the educational needs of the youth of Tangub City and the surrounding barrios through its junior and senior high school programs. In line with this mission, the school is committed to provide each student with learning experiences so that the end he/she may:

1. Be prepared for whatever course he/she may pursue after high school by means of well-rounded academic and vocational preparation for adult life through planned curricular and co-curricular programs

2. Manifest attitude of a well-integrated mature personality deeply imbued with human values.

3. Demonstrate citizenship and genuine pride to his/her cultural heritage as Filipino who is conscious of the world situation in general and in the country in particular.
